# synthesis template

The synthesizer merges the runner candidates into one package using this structure. Do not average. Pick, justify, and keep the strongest dissent visible.

---

## Recommendation
The single approach you are recommending, stated plainly. Two or three sentences.

## Why this one
The reasoning. Reference the candidates by what they contributed. Name what you took from each.

## What was rejected and why
The approaches or choices you did not take, each with a one-line reason. This is the record. It stops the team from relitigating settled forks.

## Open forks
The real decisions that are not yours to make. Each fork states the options, the trade-off, and what you would need to decide. These feed the decision gate. Resolve observable ones yourself first and only list genuine product, stakeholder, or preference calls.

## Risks to watch
The risks that survived synthesis, and the early signal for each.

## Strongest dissent
The most credible argument against the recommendation, stated fairly. If a smart reviewer would push back, write their push-back here so it is not lost.
